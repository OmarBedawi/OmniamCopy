A Shirt From Thousands of Years Ago

The life and evolution of the shirt comprise a process that began decades in the past.
That indifferent elegance, palpable at times, when we pick up a picture of our parents when they were young.
What is now the state of the art?
In 2023, wearing a shirt is a rebellious feat, a statement:
consideration and commitment.
A perfect shirt reflects the person wearing it, suggesting its qualities to the beholder.
How?
Shirting fabrics are created through a series of unique processes that transform and make them precious, soft and lustrous. Processes such as spooling, warping, sizing and finishing.
Dozens of processes at the end of which you do not yet have a product ready for the making of cotton shirts, but a raw fabric that is checked by expert personnel, able to remove any small defects due to the raw material of natural origin.
We have redefined the concept of quality by sublimating it to the extreme, reviewing our production and material sourcing processes as a testament to our commitment to the environment.
